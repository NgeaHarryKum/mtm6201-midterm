IMMUNE HEALTH

is important when it comes to staying healthy – not only during the chilly winter months, but yearround. An 8 fl oz glass of orange juice naturally contains the antioxidant, vitamin C that helps support the immune system. Simply Orange Juice with calcium contains vitamin D, a nutrient also essential to maintain immune health.

Vitamin C

This important antioxidant supports a healthy immune system. It also helps support collagen production which promotes healthy skin, hair and nails. A glass of orange juice is an excellent source of Vitamin C. In fact, citrus juices are reported to be the largest contributor of Vitamin C in people’s diets.

Vitamin D

This vitamin also helps support the body’s immune system and function. For our Simply Orange with Calcium and Vitamin D, we take mother nature and then enhance a glass of Simply Orange with Calcium and Vitamin D to deliver additional nutrition.

BONE HEALTH

This mineral helps build and maintain strong bones and teeth.

Vitamin D

This vitamin helps the body absorb calcium for strong and healthy bones.

SUPPORTING HEALTHY SYSTEMS

means keeping your body healthy from the inside out. The good news is, a simple glass of 100% orange juice provides essential nutrients you need for a healthy body.

Thiamin

This is a B-vitamin crucial for energy metabolism and is needed to help normal functioning of the heart and nervous system.

Folate

A B-vitamin that’s essential for normal cell growth and production of new cells – especially important for pregnant women.

HEART HEALTH

is important at any age. Every on the go bottle of 100% orange juice helps support heart health with simple, nutrient benefits.

Potassium

This crucial mineral supports heart and muscle function. It also helps maintain normal fluid and electrolyte balance in the body. Diets containing foods that are a good source of potassium and that are low in sodium may reduce the risk of high blood pressure and stroke.

Sodium-Free

Simply Orange is naturally sodium-free.
